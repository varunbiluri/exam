  15. FAMILY EDUCATIONAL RIGHTS AND PRIVACY ACT

    Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act ("FERPA") is a federal legislation that protects the privacy of students education records. FERPA applies to a variety of information that directly relates to the student and is maintained by the educational institution or an outside agency such as Socratease. We handle all student data in compliance with FERPA regulations.

    While not all collected information is to be treated with the same scrutiny, an educational record, such as a course name, assessment grades or audio and video sessions of a proctored exam, are highly protected.

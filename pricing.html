When a student successfully submits a test, we count it as a Test Attempt. For example, if there are 30 students in your class and they submit 2 tests each, the total number of Test Attempts is 30 x 2 = 60.

Once you become a subscriber, you can always buy more Test Attempts. We call this topping up your subscription. The cost is for 150 Test Attempts.

If you want multiple users to access the same account, you can do so by purchasing the Premium Subscription. With this, you can create a Team on AutoProctor. You can then add and exclude members from your Team.

If you have a business email ID (for example, one ending with @school.com) and a Premium subscription, all other users whose email IDs end with @school.com can access your account. You can further restrict who within this set of users can access your account via whitelisting and blacklisting users.
